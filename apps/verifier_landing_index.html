Editorial principles

News that puts truth first.

The Verifier is designed to separate reporting, evidence, commentary, and uncertainty so readers can understand not only what is being claimed, but how the claim is supported.

Source transparency

Distinguish primary sources, official records, credible reporting, commentary, and unresolved claims.

Accountability

Correct meaningful errors, identify conflicts of interest, and avoid presenting opinion as established fact.

Reader independence

Provide enough context for readers to inspect evidence and reach informed conclusions of their own.
