Editorial standards

News that puts truth first.

The Verifier separates evidence, reporting, commentary, and uncertainty.

Source transparency

Identify primary sources, official records, credible reporting, commentary, and unresolved claims.

Accountability

Correct meaningful errors, disclose relevant conflicts, and never present opinion as established fact.

Reader independence

Provide enough context for readers to inspect evidence and reach informed conclusions of their own.

“The goal of a constitutional republic is not to eliminate disagreement. The goal is to create a system where disagreement can happen peacefully, where power changes hands without violence, and where citizens remain the ultimate source of authority.”Jennifer Kay Pearl
