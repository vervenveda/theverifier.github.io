Editorial standards

News that puts truth first.

The Verifier separates evidence, reporting, commentary, uncertainty, and unresolved claims.

Source transparency

Identify primary records, official sources, credible reporting, commentary, and meaningful gaps in the available evidence.

Accountability

Correct meaningful errors, disclose relevant conflicts, and never present opinion or inference as established fact.

Reader independence

Provide enough context and source access for readers to inspect evidence and reach informed conclusions of their own.
