Editorial standards

News that puts truth first.

The Verifier separates evidence, reporting, commentary, and uncertainty so readers can understand what is known, what is claimed, and how each conclusion is supported.

Source transparency

Identify primary sources, official records, credible reporting, commentary, and unresolved claims.

Accountability

Correct meaningful errors, disclose relevant conflicts, and never present opinion as established fact.

Reader independence

Provide enough context for readers to inspect evidence and reach informed conclusions of their own.
